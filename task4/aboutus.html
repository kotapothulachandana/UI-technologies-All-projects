shinchan multi-store model is an explanation of memory proposed by Atkinson and Shiffrin which assumes there are three unitary (separate) memory stores, and that information is transferred between these stores in a linear sequence.

Multi-store as the name suggests is sellers creating or having multiple stores online to sell their products. The majority of reasons why sellers create multiple stores is to target different target markets or to sell different products under different pricing models and brands.
